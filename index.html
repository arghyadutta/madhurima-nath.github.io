Welcome!

I am interested in understanding the structural and dynamical properties of complex systems. Self-driven, dedicated data & analytics professional with great technical, business and leadership skills. I have 10+ years of advanced analytics experience and specialize in NLP and data engineering. I work on developing and deploying AI/ML solutions on cloud platforms for clients. Personally, I love solving challenging problems which gives me opportunity to learn and grow. I am also an active member of Women in Network Science, Women in Machine Learning & Data Science and Women Who Code communities.

Prior to Slalom, I worked on developing algorithms to analyze complex interacting systems and I graduated with a PhD in computational physics from Virginia Tech Dec. 2018, where I worked on Application of Network Reliability to Analyze Diffusive Processes on Graph Dynamical Systems with Prof. Stephen Eubank.
