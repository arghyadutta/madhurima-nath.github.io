Arghya Dutta

I am a physicist with a keen interest in material design. Material design becomes easier with expressive descriptors; I use data-driven methods to identify them—frequently leveraging techniques from machine learning, numerical simulations, and statistical mechanics. We discovered descriptors in several intriguing forms, including coarse-grained sequence patterns, equations, and reduced vector embeddings. Check out my latest papers if you're interested!

I work as a postdoctoral researcher at Goethe University. [CV, Twitter, Email: argphy@gmail.com]
